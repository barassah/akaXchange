Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The exchange program is open to students enrolled at the participating academies in Mombasa, Maputo, and Hyderabad. Eligibility criteria may vary slightly between academies, but generally include academic performance, language proficiency, and demonstrated interest in cultural exchange.

Participating students gain valuable cultural understanding, academic enrichment, and global networking opportunities. They also develop language skills, broaden their perspectives, and enhance their resume with international experience.

The duration of the exchange program, typically ranges from three months. Specific timelines and schedules will be provided upon acceptance into the program.

Students are selected based on academic merit, language proficiency, personal statements or essays, and recommendations from faculty members. Each academy may have its own selection committee and criteria.

Academic credits earned during the exchange program are typically transferable back to your home academy, subject to approval by academic advisors and relevant departments. It is important to consult with your academic advisor prior to departure.

The exchange program may cover expenses such as tuition fees at the host academy, accommodation arrangements, and sometimes a stipend for living expenses. Specific details on financial support will be provided during the application process.
